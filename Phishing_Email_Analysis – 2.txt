Phishing Email Analysis Report – Example-2

Subject: Content Infringement on @Novacorp
Sender: Seb from CanIPhish
Date of Email: Wed 3 Jan 2024
Decision Date Claimed: Wed May 28 2025

Phishing Indicators:

1. Impersonation – Email uses X (Twitter) branding, misleading the recipient.
2. Suspicious Sender – Claimed sender is not a valid domain related to X/Twitter.
3. Urgent Language – Legal warning to force the recipient to click or act.
4. Missing Company Info – No contact details or footer, which is unprofessional.
5. Phishing Intent – The email likely contains a fake link (cut off in image) aiming to steal credentials.

Recommendation: Never click on suspicious links. Always verify sender identity and check URLs before responding.
